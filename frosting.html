Tangy Greek Yogurt Frosting

By Daniel Wagman | July, 2022
A must-try frosting recipe!

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups (475 g) greek yogurt (2% fat)
  2. Small pinch salt
  3. 3 tablespoons (60 g) honey
  4. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix the greek yogurt and salt in a medium-sized bowl. Pour into a large piece of cheesecloth hung over a large bowl. Refridgerate overnight.
  2. Now, the greek yogurt mixture should be noticably thicker and creamier. Transfer to a large bowl. Add honey and vanilla extract. Whip for 1-2 minutes, or until well-combined, light, and fluffy.
  3. Taste some! Add honey, salt, and/or vanilla to taste.
  4. Enjoy!
