Cozy Apple Crumb Cake

By Daniel Wagman | October, 2021
It is not fall, so I am quite unsure why I am publishing this recipe now.

Ingredients

  • Crumb topping:
    1. 1/4 cup (30 g) white whole-wheat flour
    2. Pinch kosher salt
    3. 2 ½ teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
    4. 2 tablespoons (25g) granulated sugar
    5. 2 tablespoons (25g) neutral oil (such as canola or vegetable)
    6. ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Cake batter:
    1. 1 ½ cups (180 g) white whole-wheat flour
    2. 1 teaspoon baking soda
    3. ½ teaspoon sea salt
    4. 2 ½ teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
    5. 1 heaping cup (about 250g) grated apples (not peeled; for me, that was 2 medium-large apples)
    6. 1/2 cup (100 g) neutral oil (such as canola or vegetable)
    7. 1/4 cup (50 g) brown sugar
    8. 1/4 cup (75 g) maple syrup
    9. 1/2 cup (120 g) unsweetened applesauce
    10. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and line an 8 by 8 baking pan with parchment paper. Add all crumb topping ingredients to a small bowl and mix well until fully combined. Set aside. Add flour, baking soda, sea salt, and pumpkin pie spice to a medium-sized bowl. Whisk together until well combined. Add grated apples, oil, applesauce, sugar, and vanilla extract to another medium-sized bowl and whisk to combine. Pour wet ingredients into the dry mix and mix together with a rubber spatula until combined, but do not overmix. Make sure to incorporate the flour on the sides and bottom of the bowl. Pour batter into the prepared pan. Sprinkle crumb topping on top. Bake until done and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 30-40 minutes. Set the pan on a cooling rack and let cool for an hour. Enjoy!
