Plum-Blueberry Crisp with Cornmeal Crumble

By Daniel Wagman | June, 2022
A great option for summertime barbeques–enjoy!

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound (450 grams) plums, washed, dried, pitted, and cut into ½ inch cubes
  2. 1 pound (450 grams) blueberries, washed and dried
  3. 5 tablespoons (65 grams) granulated sugar, divided
  4. 4 tablespoons (50 grams) extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  5. ¼ cup (30 grams) white whole-wheat flour
  6. ¼ cup (30 grams) whole-grain cornmeal, fine or medium grind
  7. Pinch kosher salt
  8. ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Add the plums, blueberries, 3 tablespoons (40 grams) of the granulated sugar, and 2 tablespoons (25 grams) of the extra-virgin olive oil to an 8x8 baking dish. Toss to combine and evenly coat the fruit. In a small bowl, add the flour, cornmeal, kosher salt, vanilla extract, remaining 2 tablespoons of (25 grams) granulated sugar, and remaining 2 tablespoons (25 grams) of extra-virgin olive oil. Mix with a fork to combine. Sprinkle the cornmeal mixture over the fruit mixture. Bake for 50-60 minutes. Let cool for 5 minutes, if desired. Enjoy!
